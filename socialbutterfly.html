About Project

Description

Social Butterfly is an app that brings a platform to make connections with common interests and events, which lead to meeting new people.The app is specially made for those users who want to teach or learn new things and meet new people around them. It brings different people together around the world to do, explore, teach, and learn the thing that helps them come alive.

Problem

People who are currently quarantined due to COVID-19 need to feel socially connected and engaged with family, friends, and other people with similar interests in a virtual setting because with the lack of ability to meet in person, this desire for socialization has not disappeared.

Main Goal

Our goal is to better understand how people make connections and interactive experiences virtually with other people.

Research

We began by hypothesizing that people have been looking for resources and tools to connect with their current friends in a more interactive way, while still social distancing. And regardless of the pandemic, people still had the desire to make connections with new people.

User Interviews

We started our research by interviewing 5 users from the ages of 18-55 to see how the Covid-19 pandemic impact their new and current connections. Following our user interviews we conducted a survey based on the interview questions.
