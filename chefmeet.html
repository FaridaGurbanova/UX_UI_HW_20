About Project

Description

ChefMeet is a healthy food delivery service that offers nutritionally balanced and freshly prepared food. They target young working professionals and busy individuals who can trust ChefMeet to achieve a healthier lifestyle. Chefmeet is connecting hungry business professionals to everyday chefs in your own neighborhood.

Problem

Nowadays life is very busy and doesn’t allow people time to cook homemade authentic meals. Not only is the problem for consumers, but also individuals who do cook, they are making too much food and end up throwing away the leftovers.

Solution

The Solution is enabling neighborhood chefs to share their cooking skills with others. This application gives the user the convenience and variety of culturally diverse home-cooked meals nearby.Our app allows the consumer to donate any excess/additional servings to a donation/shelter/family in need.

Final Thoughts

ChefMeet Will Alleviate Stress:

Surrounding grocery shopping, meal planning, ensuring a healthy and nutritious diet for the entire family to love and enjoy. Creating a seamless and personalized meal planning experience without requiring any extra time, skills or tedious planning from the user.

Nothing Like It Currently:

There is not an existing application allowing users to search and connect with personal chefs, despite the large demand to simplify cooking, grocery shopping, and meal prep.

The Solution:

ChefMeet will allow busy professionals to easily and efficiently search for a personal chef’s. Filtering by reviews, price, dietary restrictions, and categories. Saving the user’s personalized filters, information, restrictions/requirements, and payment information for future use. As for the chefs, ChefMeet will showcase the Chef’s certifications, reviews, experience and provide a secure background check.

Looking Ahead:

ChefMeet will potentially make profit additionally, from ads that will periodically show at login. Also, offering a monthly subscription option to encourage the loyalty and longevity of the user. This will provide the user with exclusive offers and pricing.
